UX Case Study: American Clothing Pickup

Project Type:Non-Profit Web Design Year: 2024 Role: UI Designer, Visual Designer and UX Researcher.

American Clothing Pickup is a Non-Profit Organization focused on helping families in need who accept all clothing, shoes, handbags, and textiles like blankets, sheets, towels, curtains, toys and small housewares.

The Problem

American Clothing Pick Up aims to provide an efficient donation process, but users struggle with unclear instructions, inefficient navigation, and accessibility issues. Which makes the donation process more tedious than it should be.

Problem We Solved

As a UX/UI researcher and designer, we prioritize simplifying the American Clothing Pick Up website to enhance user experience, focusing on clear instructions, intuitive navigation, and improved accessibility for a seamless donation process.

Design Process

First step, was conduction review on the website to see what component was usable and what new ideas we can incorporate to make the user experience more enjoyable and unforgettable. Then we proceed to the interview process which gave us a scope on the user experience which allows to fulfilled their needs.

Research

These pie chart are results from our survey we conducted to get insight on people motivation to give back, donation history, the importance of tracking where their donation is being used for, which method they use when donating.
